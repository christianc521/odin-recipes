CREAMY PESTO MAC WITH SPINACH

pasta so creamy, it'll sound like last nights regret. add in shredded chicken for those extra gains.

WHAT YOULL NEED

  • 2 cups uncooked macaroni ($0.50)
  • 2 Tbsp butter ($0.22)
  • 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour ($0.02)
  • 2 cups whole milk ($0.60)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan ($0.53)
  • 1/4 cup basil pesto ($0.55)
  • 1/2 tsp salt ($0.02)
  • Freshly cracked pepper ($0.03)
  • 1/2 lb. frozen spinach ($0.80)

TOTAL COST = $3.27 FOR 6 SERVINGS

HOW TO MAKE IT

  1. thaw spinach and squeeze out excess water. boil macaroni for 7-10 mins.
  2. add butter and flour to small sauce pot over medium heat. stir until sauce gets creamy and bubbly. lol. heat for another min.
  3. whisk in milk until simmer. when sauce is thick, remove from heat.
  4. whisk in parmesan and pesto until smooth. season with salt and pepper.
  5. drain pasta. add in spinach. pour sauce on top.
  6. feast.
