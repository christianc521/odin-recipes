CURRIED CHICKPEAS WITH SPINACH

WHAT YOULL NEED

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil ($0.26)
  • 1 yellow onion ($0.47)
  • 2 cloves garlic ($0.16)
  • 1 inch fresh ginger ($0.16)
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp curry powder ($0.45)
  • 8 oz. spinach (fresh or frozen) ($1.49)
  • 2 15oz. cans chickpeas ($1.10)

TOTAL COST = $4.68 FOR 4 SERVINGS

HOW TO MAKE IT

  1. dice onion, mice garlic, grate ginger. sauté with olive oil on medium heat until onions are soft.
  2. add curry powder and sauté for another minute. add 1/4 cup of water with spinach. sauté until spinach looks limp.
  3. rinse chickpeas. add to skillet with tomato sauce. stir and cook for another 5 mins.
  4. serve over rice or with bread.
